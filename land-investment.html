Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our land investment opportunities

What types of land do you offer?

We offer various types of land including residential plots, commercial plots, and agricultural land. Each type is carefully selected based on location, growth potential, and legal status to ensure the best investment opportunity for our members.

What documentation will I receive?

Upon completing your payment, you will receive a comprehensive documentation package including a Deed of Assignment, Survey Plan, and Receipt of Payment. For certain properties, we also provide Certificate of Occupancy (C of O) or Governor's Consent documentation depending on the land's status.

How flexible are your payment plans?

We offer highly flexible payment plans tailored to different financial capacities. Options include outright purchase with discount, 3-month, 6-month, and 12-month installment plans. For commercial properties and larger acquisitions, we can create customized payment structures to accommodate your specific needs.

Can I visit the properties before investing?

We encourage all potential investors to visit and inspect our properties before making a commitment. We organize regular site visits and can also arrange private tours upon request. This allows you to see the land, assess its location, and make an informed investment decision.

What happens if I can't complete my payments?

We understand that financial circumstances can change. If you're unable to complete your payments according to the agreed schedule, please contact us as soon as possible. We can work with you to restructure your payment plan or explore other options. Our goal is to help you succeed in your investment journey.
