Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting and working with us

What is the best way to contact SunRise Cooperative?

The best way to contact us depends on your needs. For quick inquiries, you can call our customer service line at +234 800 123 4567. For detailed questions or proposals, email us at info@sunrisecooperative.com. If you prefer face-to-face interaction, you can visit our office during business hours. We also respond promptly to messages sent through our contact form on this page.

How long does it take to get a response?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For phone calls, you'll get immediate assistance during our office hours. Email and contact form submissions are typically responded to within one business day. Complex inquiries that require consultation with specific departments may take up to 48 hours for a comprehensive response.

Can I schedule a meeting with a representative?

Yes, you can schedule a meeting with our representatives. To arrange a meeting, please call our office or send an email with your preferred date and time, along with a brief description of the topics you'd like to discuss. We offer both in-person meetings at our office and virtual consultations via video conferencing platforms for your convenience.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations through various platforms including Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. Virtual consultations are a convenient option for clients who cannot visit our office in person or who are located outside Akwa Ibom State. To schedule a virtual consultation, please contact us via email or phone, and we'll arrange a suitable time and provide the necessary meeting details.

How can I join SunRise Cooperative Society?

To join SunRise Cooperative Society, you can start by filling out our membership application form, which is available on our website or at our office. After submitting the form along with the required documentation and initial contribution, our membership committee will review your application. Once approved, you'll receive a welcome package with your membership details and information about the benefits and opportunities available to you as a member.
