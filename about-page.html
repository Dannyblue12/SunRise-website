About SunRise Cooperative

Empowering communities through sustainable development and cooperative growth since our inception

Our Journey

SunRise Multipurpose Cooperative Society Ltd. was founded with a vision to create sustainable economic opportunities for communities in Akwa Ibom State and beyond. Our cooperative model harnesses the power of collective resources to drive meaningful development and prosperity.

Since our inception, we have been committed to fostering growth across multiple sectors, including real estate, agriculture, and business development, all while maintaining our core values of integrity, sustainability, and community empowerment.

DR. Obong Umanaette Udoh FCA

Founder & President, SunRise Multi-Purpose

Former Accountant General Of Akwa-Ibom State

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

At Sunrise Multipurpose Cooperative Society Ltd., we envision a future where economic prosperity and natural wellness go hand in hand. We see communities thriving through sustainable development, where the benefits of cooperative growth are shared equitably among all members.

We aspire to be the leading cooperative society in Nigeria, recognized for our innovative approach to community development, environmental stewardship, and financial empowerment.

Our Mission

By harnessing the power of cooperative collaboration, we aim to uplift communities, empower farmers, and create sustainable opportunities for our members in Akwa Ibom and across the diaspora.

Our commitment extends beyond financial growth—we strive to promote the richness of organic, health-enhancing substances, ensuring that their medicinal and nutritional benefits reach people worldwide.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our operations and relationships

Integrity

We operate with honesty, transparency, and ethical standards in all our dealings, building trust with our members and partners.

Sustainability

We are committed to environmentally responsible practices that ensure long-term viability and preserve resources for future generations.

Community Empowerment

We believe in the power of collective action to transform lives and communities, creating opportunities for growth and prosperity.
