Who Are We?

At Sunrise Multipurpose Cooperative Society Ltd., we envision a future where economic prosperity and natural wellness go hand in hand. By harnessing the power of cooperative collaboration, we aim to uplift communities, empower farmers, and create sustainable opportunities for our members in Akwa Ibom and across the diaspora.

Our commitment extends beyond financial growth—we strive to promote the richness of organic, health-enhancing substances, ensuring that their medicinal and nutritional benefits reach people worldwide.

Our Vision

To cultivate a sustainable future where agricultural prosperity and environmental stewardship thrive in harmony.

Our Mission

To empower communities through sustainable agriculture, innovative financial solutions, and eco-friendly practices.

Our Core Values

Integrity, sustainability, and community empowerment guide our commitment to fostering growth and prosperity.
