Who Are We?

At Sunrise Multipurpose Cooperative Society Ltd., we envision a future where economic prosperity and natural wellness go hand in hand. By harnessing the power of cooperative collaboration, we aim to uplift communities, empower farmers, and create sustainable opportunities for our members in Akwa Ibom and across the diaspora.

Our commitment extends beyond financial growthwe strive to promote the richness of organic, health-enhancing substances, ensuring that their medicinal and nutritional benefits reach people worldwide.

Our Vision

To cultivate a sustainable future where agricultural prosperity and environmental stewardship thrive in harmony.

Our Mission

To empower communities through sustainable agriculture, innovative financial solutions, and eco-friendly practices.

Our Core Values

Integrity, sustainability, and community empowerment guide our commitment to fostering growth and prosperity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our investment opportunities

Is there any land available in Uyo?
Yes, we currently have premium land available in Uyo, including our flagship Diasporas Estate development next to Ibom Hotels and our Mbiokporo Estate along Etinan Road. Both locations offer excellent investment potential with secure documentation.
How much is an hectare of land leased?
The cost of leasing a hectare of land varies depending on the location, accessibility, and development potential. Our current rates for agricultural land leases start from 500,000 per hectare annually. For specific pricing on commercial or residential land leases, please contact our team directly.
How do I get to own one of these lands?
The process is straightforward: First, contact us to express your interest. Our team will provide detailed information about available properties. Once you've selected a property, you can secure it with an initial deposit, followed by flexible payment options. We handle all documentation and legal processes to ensure a smooth transfer of ownership.
What payment options are available for investors?
We offer flexible payment options including initial deposits with installment plans, full payment discounts, and custom financing solutions. For diaspora investors, we have special arrangements to facilitate international transactions and ensure a smooth investment process regardless of your location.
What documentation will I receive after purchase?
Upon completing your payment, you will receive comprehensive documentation including a Deed of Assignment, Survey Plan, and Receipt of Payment. We also assist with the process of obtaining a Certificate of Occupancy (C of O) where applicable, ensuring your investment is fully secured with all necessary legal documentation.
